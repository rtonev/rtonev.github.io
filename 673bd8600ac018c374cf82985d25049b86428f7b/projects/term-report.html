Summary

I participated in a Hackathon hosted by the University of Michigan, which was geared towards staff employees. Our team designed and developed a solution to help support super computers. The solution allowed users to make a screencast of their session and send it to support stafff, which can later view it using a web app. We won the competition!

Problem Definition

Researchers access super computers via terminal clients. When they encounter a technical problem, they call the IT support desk and ask for their assistance. Since it is hard to remember the exact commands that they entered on the terminal, it is very hard for researchers to accurately communicate what sequence of commands led to their problem. To help support staff solve problems faster, we developed a tool that lets researchers record their screen and send that recording to the support staff. Additionally, we also hooked up the sending of the recording to the ticketing system, as well as developed a web app that allows IT support staff to view the terminal sessions recordings.
