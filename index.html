ABOUT ME

Raised in a family of artists, I quickly developed a great appreciation for art and aesthetics. My desire to solve problems and impact people’s lives, however, led me in a different direction. Captivated by the age of computers, I instead followed my passion for technology and became a software engineer. Nevertheless, during my five years of work, I was unfulfilled and wanted to re-engage my creative side.

I decided to pursue a path in UX design, where I am now combining my coding and artistic talents, working relentlessly to solve problems and enhance the user experience. Currently I am advancing these skills at the University of Michigan's School of Information, where I am a Master's student specializing in Human-Computer Interaction.
