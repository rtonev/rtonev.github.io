Summary

I was tasked with designing an app for Gracie Barra Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Association, with over 800 schools on six continents. The app was focused on e-learning, as well as building an online community, that would break the physical barriers and connect all students in the Gracie Barra family. The app featured an experience that allowed studesnts to learn, interact with each other, and recieve support on their journeys.

Problem statement

Gracie Barra is one of the greatest brands in the world of martial arts. They have a culture and long tradition of being one big family. Throughout the years, they had enjoyed a great community among its memebers. But as digital technologies became widespread, Gracie Barra needed to evolve as well. My goal was to build a digital community of practice, focused around learning and building relationships.

Competitive analysis

Since I was new to the domain, I explored similar products to find inspiration. I focused on e-learning and digital community platforms in the fitness space, as those apps had many of the characteristics that were importnat to me.


To-be user journey

After conducting my analysis, i started thinking about the overall user journey. This helped me focus on the big picture, before diving into the details. I was also able to validate this journey with my client, to make sure that I am not missing key steps or have an incorrect order.


Sketches

Next, I started brainstorming by sketching. At this point, I was laying out my thoughts into concrete screen ideas. In this phase, I explored concepts for different features, as well as various interaction models.


Mid-fi prototype

As I started making my ideas more concrete and dove into more details, I created a mid-fi prototype. I used this prototype to get a better sense of the space usage and interactions of my design. I also used this prototype to get feedback from my client and iterate.


Hi-fi prototype

Finally, I created a hi-fi prototype that also incorporated the Gracie Barra branding. I aimed to capture the spirit of the famed Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu school, while designing a mobile-native application. A key area of focus was the community aspect of the app, which I found to be at the cetner of the Gracie Barra organization.
