Summary

As a hobby, I created an iOS puzzle game. The game is logically challenging, while also striving to be fun and entertaining. The objective of the game is to help a lost balloon find their way out of a ventilation system.

Gameplay

The player guides the balloon by making light taps. When a light tap is visible, the balloon follows the light. The balloon will go to the exit if it sees it, but there are many dangerous obstacles on the way...

Characters

There are 9 balloon characters that users can pick from. This lets users customize the game to their taste and style.

Sounds

Each character has their own voice and personality. I worked with actors and friends to do the voice recordings an make sure that each balloon was voiced uniquely.
