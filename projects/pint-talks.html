Summary

I designed the branding for the Pint Talks Podcast. My work included picking typefaces colors, as well as designing a logo. I also worked on variety of assets for social media, which included designing posts, audiograms and banners.

Pint Talks Podcast

Pint Talks is a podcast about science, technology, and how they imapact society. The show format features two pals that sit down and have a discussion over a pint. The brand is about filtering serious topics through the lens of a casual and friendly conversation by two non-experts.

Typeface

For typefaces, I picked Anton for headings and Open Sans for body text. The types intended to communicate a clean, modern look that is visually engaging, while also maintaining a sense of simplicity.


Color

For colors, I was inspired by the colors of hops. I strived to establish a connection to a pint of beer, as well as to nature in general. I was attracted by the connection of green colors to nature as I feel like that can make the brand my earthy and easier to digest.
